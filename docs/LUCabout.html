Who We Are

Our Mission Statement

Blockchain@LUC’s mission is to spread awareness of blockchain technology, its history, its continuing impact, and relevant projects. In maintaining this focus, we strive to educate members with a quality understanding of what this space offers, creating leaders who can use their knowledge to seamlessly adapt to the inevitable transition to a fully digitized world as the Information Age progresses.
